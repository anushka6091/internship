What is EV BATTERY SWAPPING?

EV battery swapping is a method of quickly replacing the depleted battery
of an electric vehicle (EV) with a fully charged one, instead of waiting to recharge it.


BENEFITS

  • Drive to a battery swapping station.
  • The empty battery is removed (manually or automatically).
  • A fully charged battery is inserted into your EV.
  • You're back on the road in under 5 minutes.

ADVANTAGES

  • Zero downtime for commercial vehicles.
  • No need to wait at charging stations.
  • Lower EV cost (battery is a major part of EV price).
  • Centralized battery maintenance and recycling

DISADVANTAGES

  • Requires standardized battery designs across EVs.
  • Expensive infrastructure and logistics.
  • Concerns about battery ownership and compatibility.
  • Needs a network of well-maintained stations
